AcmeTech Solutions Employee Data Policy
1. Purpose
This policy establishes the requirements for collecting, processing, storing, and protecting employee
personal data at AcmeTech Solutions.
2. Scope
This policy applies to all employees, contractors, interns, and temporary staff whose personal information is
processed by AcmeTech Solutions.
3. Data Collected
AcmeTech may collect:
Full name
Address
Phone number
Email address
Government identification number
Payroll information
Attendance records
Performance review records
4. Lawful Basis for Processing
Employee data shall only be processed for legitimate business purposes including:
Payroll administration
Benefits management
Employment contracts
Legal compliance obligations
5. Data Security
Employee personal data shall be protected through:
Role-based access controls
Encryption at rest
Encryption in transit
Multi-factor authentication
Periodic security audits

6. Data Retention
Employee records shall be retained for seven years following termination of employment unless a longer
retention period is required by law.
7. Employee Rights
Employees have the right to:
Access personal information
Correct inaccurate information
Request deletion where legally permissible
Restrict processing in certain circumstances
8. International Data Transfers
Employee data may only be transferred outside the European Union when adequate safeguards are in
place.
9. Responsibilities
Human Resources is responsible for ensuring compliance with this policy.
The Information Security Team is responsible for implementing technical safeguards.
