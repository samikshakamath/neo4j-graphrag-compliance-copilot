AcmeTech Solutions Data Deletion Procedure
1. Purpose
This procedure defines the process for handling requests to delete personal data held by AcmeTech
Solutions. The objective is to ensure compliance with applicable privacy regulations and company policies
while maintaining appropriate records for legal, regulatory, and operational purposes.
2. Scope
This procedure applies to:
Customer personal data
Employee personal data
Vendor representative personal data
Any personal information processed by AcmeTech Solutions
This procedure shall be followed by all departments handling personal data.
3. Roles and Responsibilities
Data Subject
The individual requesting deletion of personal information.
Customer Support Team
Responsible for receiving and documenting deletion requests from customers.
Human Resources Department
Responsible for handling employee-related deletion requests.
Data Protection Officer (DPO)
Responsible for reviewing deletion requests and ensuring regulatory compliance.
Information Security Team
Responsible for executing approved deletion activities and maintaining audit records.

4. Deletion Request Channels
Requests may be submitted through:
Customer support portal
Privacy request form
Official company email address
Human Resources service desk
All requests must be assigned a unique request identifier.
5. Deletion Request Workflow
Step 1: Request Submission
The requester submits a deletion request containing:
Full name
Contact information
Description of the request
Proof of identity where required
Step 2: Identity Verification
The company shall verify the identity of the requester before processing any deletion request.
Verification methods may include:
Account authentication
Government-issued identification
Employment verification
Step 3: Request Registration
The request shall be logged in the Privacy Request Register.
The following information shall be recorded:
Request ID
Request date
Request type
Requester information
Assigned reviewer

Step 4: Eligibility Assessment
The Data Protection Officer shall determine whether the deletion request can be fulfilled.
Deletion may be denied when:
Legal obligations require retention
Ongoing litigation exists
Financial record retention requirements apply
Security investigations are active
Step 5: Approval
Approved requests shall be forwarded to the Information Security Team for execution.
Rejected requests shall be documented with justification.
Step 6: Data Deletion Execution
The Information Security Team shall remove personal information from:
Production databases
Customer management systems
Human resource systems
File storage repositories
Analytics platforms
Where complete deletion is not technically possible, data shall be anonymized.
Step 7: Verification
A secondary reviewer shall verify that deletion activities were completed successfully.
Verification shall include:
Database checks
Application record checks
Storage validation
Step 8: Closure
The requester shall be notified of completion.
The request status shall be marked as closed.

6. Retention Exceptions
Data shall not be deleted if retention is required for:
Tax regulations
Employment law obligations
Fraud investigations
Legal proceedings
Regulatory audits
The reason for retention must be documented.
7. Processing Timeline
Deletion requests shall be acknowledged within 5 business days.
Approved deletion requests should be completed within 30 calendar days.
Complex requests may require additional review and approval.
8. Audit Logging
The following information shall be maintained for audit purposes:
Request ID
Processing dates
Approval decisions
Systems affected
Personnel involved
Audit records shall be retained for five years.
9. Security Requirements
Deletion activities shall be performed only by authorized personnel.
Administrative actions must be logged and monitored.
All deletion activities shall follow company information security standards.
