AcmeTech Solutions Customer Privacy Policy
1. Purpose
This policy explains how AcmeTech collects, processes, and protects customer personal information.
2. Information Collected
The company may collect:
Name
Email address
Phone number
Billing information
Device information
Website usage information
3. Purpose of Processing
Customer data is processed for:
Service delivery
Customer support
Billing
Security monitoring
Legal compliance
4. Consent Management
Where required, customer consent shall be obtained before collecting or processing personal information.
Customers may withdraw consent at any time.
5. Data Sharing
Customer information may be shared with approved service providers who perform services on behalf of
AcmeTech.
All third-party processors must sign a Data Processing Agreement.

6. Customer Rights
Customers may request:
Access to personal information
Correction of inaccurate information
Deletion of personal information
Export of personal information
7. Security Controls
AcmeTech protects customer data using:
Encryption
Access controls
Audit logging
Continuous monitoring
8. International Transfers
Transfers of customer information outside the European Union require approved legal transfer
mechanisms.
9. Data Retention
Customer records shall be retained for five years after account closure unless legal requirements dictate
otherwise.
